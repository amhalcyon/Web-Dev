Travel to Paris

Paris, capital of France, is one of the most important and influential cities in the world. In terms of tourism, Paris is the second most visited city in Europe after London. In this webpage, you’ll find out about the city’s top attractions.

Top 5 sights in Paris:

  1. Eiffel Tower



    2. The Eiffel Tower was designed for the Exposition Universelle, a world fair held in Paris in 1889. It is currently the most famous symbol of Paris.

  2. Louvre Museum



    3. Opened at the end of the eighteenth century, the Louvre is the country’s most renowned museum and one of the most visited in the world.

  3. Notre Dame Cathedral



    4. Built between 1163 and 1245, Notre Dame de Paris is one of the oldest gothic cathedrals in the world. Find out more about this magnificent cathedral.

  4. Arc de Triomphe



    5. The Arc de Triomphe is one of the main symbols of Paris. The construction of the monument began in 1806 commissioned by Napoleon.

  5. Latin Quarter



    6. The Paris Latin Quarter (Quartier Latin) is one of the oldest in the city. It is currently one of the most charming and lively areas in Paris.
